Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Our Philosophy

Aurasphere (operated by Black Diamond LTD, UIC: 207807571, Sofia, Bulgaria) believes in digital sovereignty. We collect as little data as possible — because your data belongs to you, not us.

2. What We Collect

When you use Aurasphere, we collect only what's essential:

  • Email address (for login and communication)
  • Basic profile info (name, if provided)
  • Usage data (e.g., which apps you use) — stored locally or encrypted

We do NOT track you across the web. We do NOT use cookies for ads.

3. How We Use It

Your data is used only to:

  • Provide and improve Aurasphere services
  • Send essential notifications (e.g., billing, security)
  • Respond to your support requests

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share it if:

  • Required by law (e.g., Bulgarian legal request)
  • You explicitly authorize it (e.g., exporting your CRM contacts)

5. Security

All data is protected via:

  • Firebase Auth (Google-grade security)
  • Encrypted storage (Firestore & local)
  • Zero third-party analytics

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account (Settings → Delete)
  • Contact us: hello@aura-sphere.app
