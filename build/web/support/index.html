Support Center

Get help with Aurasphere products and services

How do I get started with Aurasphere?

Visit our homepage and click on your desired product (CRM Dashboard, AuraPost, AuraLink, or AuraShield). Create an account with your email and follow the onboarding wizard. For detailed guides, check the product documentation or contact our support team.

What languages are supported?

Aurasphere supports 19 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Korean, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, Turkish, Dutch, Swedish, and Norwegian.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months, but you can restart your subscription at any time.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All Aurasphere products use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256), SSL/TLS protocols, and comply with GDPR and CCPA standards. Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers with automated backups and redundancy.

Do you offer team collaboration features?

Yes! The CRM Dashboard includes comprehensive team management with role-based access control (RBAC), activity logs, and real-time synchronization. Share projects, track expenses, and collaborate on invoices seamlessly.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. Export all your data to PDF or CSV formats at any time. This is useful for backups, analysis, or integration with other tools. Your data always remains yours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! You can try the CRM Dashboard free for 14 days with full feature access. No credit card required. AuraPost, AuraLink, and AuraShield will have trial access when launched.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within seconds. Click the link, set a new password, and log in.

Do you offer API access?

Yes. For enterprise customers, we provide REST API documentation and webhooks for custom integrations. Contact our sales team to discuss API access requirements.

What is your uptime guarantee?

Aurasphere guarantees 99.9% uptime for all services. We have redundant servers, automated failover systems, and continuous monitoring. Enterprise plans include priority support.

How do I report a bug or security issue?

For bugs, contact support@aurasphere.io with details and screenshots. For security vulnerabilities, email security@aurasphere.io directly. We prioritize and respond to all security reports within 24 hours.
