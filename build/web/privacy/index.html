Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how Aurasphere ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise processes personal information in connection with our websites, applications, and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in various ways, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, company name, phone number, and billing address when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Content Data: Documents, invoices, projects, expenses, and other data you upload or create using our Services
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers)
  • Communication Data: Support tickets, emails, feedback, and survey responses
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to enhance your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process transactions and send billing information
  • Communicate about accounts, features, updates, and support
  • Personalize your experience and content recommendations
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (Firebase, Stripe, Sendgrid) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Business Partners: Integrated platforms you explicitly authorize (e.g., accounting software)
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement, regulators, or courts when legally required
  • Business Transfers: Acquirers in case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • Aggregated Data: De-identified, aggregated analytics that cannot identify you

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest; TLS/SSL for data in transit
  • Access Control: Role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication
  • Infrastructure: Secure Google Cloud & Firebase servers with automated backups
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and vulnerability assessments
  • Compliance: GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2 compliance measures

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you have rights including:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (with exceptions for legal holds)
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications anytime
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aurasphere.io.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide Services and fulfill legal obligations. You may request deletion anytime via your account settings or by contacting support. Account deletion will be processed within 30 days.

7. International Data Transfers

Aurasphere is based in Bulgaria (Sofia). If you access our Services from outside the EU/EEA, your data will be transferred to and processed in Bulgaria. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards in accordance with GDPR.

8. Children's Privacy

Aurasphere Services are not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before sharing information.

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit Service functionality.

Cookie Types:

  • Essential: Required for core functionality
  • Analytical: Help us understand usage patterns
  • Preference: Remember your settings and language choice
  • Marketing: Track campaigns and ads (requires consent)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a notice on our Services. Your continued use following notification constitutes acceptance of changes.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Email: privacy@aurasphere.io
  • Mailing Address: Black Diamond LTD, Sofia, Bulgaria
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@aurasphere.io

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

13. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

GDPR (EU Residents): We are a data controller. You have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object. Our legal basis includes contractual necessity, legitimate interest, and consent.

CCPA (California Residents): You have rights to know, delete, and opt-out of sale. We do not sell personal information. This privacy policy constitutes our CCPA disclosure.
